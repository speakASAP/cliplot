# Business Case

## Problem

`cliplot.alfares.cz` currently does not provide a working storefront experience.
Czech customers need a simple product-first path from arrival to purchase.

## Value

Cliplot can reuse existing Alfares commerce systems to reach revenue faster
without building a duplicate backend.

## Success

- Public storefront serves at `https://cliplot.alfares.cz/`.
- Customers see sellable products with price, stock, delivery, and CTA.
- Checkout starts without mandatory registration.
- Payment, stock, order, and notification evidence comes from shared systems.
