# Business: Cliplot Czech E-Commerce

## Objective

Build Cliplot into a revenue-capable Czech e-commerce storefront that reduces
friction between product discovery and home delivery.

## Audience

Primary audience:

- Czech residents;
- ages 15-65;
- mobile, laptop, and desktop users;
- modern and older devices;
- mixed levels of technical comfort.

The storefront must be clear for older users without feeling outdated for
younger buyers.

## Conversion Principles

1. Show products before brand storytelling.
2. Keep price, availability, delivery, and `Do košíku` visible.
3. Do not force registration before purchase.
4. Show final delivery/payment expectations before checkout commitment.
5. Use short Czech copy and recognizable Czech delivery/payment patterns.
6. Keep mobile fast and readable.
7. Preserve trust through firm details, return policy, secure payment, and clear
   support access.

## Trust Requirements

The storefront must make these facts easy to find:

- seller identity and IČO;
- contact method;
- return policy and Czech consumer-law copy;
- delivery providers and prices;
- payment methods;
- privacy policy and terms;
- stock and delivery estimate.
